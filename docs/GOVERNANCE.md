
Governance Boundaries
Governance Principle

ClinicalTrust evaluates AI responses before they are trusted.

The platform does not claim that an AI answer is clinically valid. It produces evidence that supports human review.

Allowed
RAG experimentation
Healthcare policy document retrieval
AI response evaluation
Evidence package generation
Reviewer workflow
Offline governance simulation
Not Allowed
Medical diagnosis
Treatment recommendations
Emergency care guidance
Processing real patient records
Production clinical decision support
Autonomous approval of medical content
